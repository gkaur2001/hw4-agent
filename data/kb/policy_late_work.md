# Late Work Policy

**Document ID:** policy_late_work.md

## Overview
All assignments have firm due dates posted in Canvas. Students are expected to submit work on time.

## Late Submission Penalties
- Submissions 1–24 hours late: **10% deduction** from the earned score.
- Submissions 25–48 hours late: **25% deduction** from the earned score.
- Submissions more than 48 hours late: **50% deduction** from the earned score.
- Submissions more than 7 days late: **not accepted** (grade of 0).

## Grace Period
A **15-minute grace period** applies to all deadlines to account for minor technical issues. Submissions within this window are considered on time.

## Graded Late Work
Late work that is accepted will be graded normally, then the deduction will be applied. For example, if you earn 90/100 but submitted 30 hours late, you receive 90 × 0.75 = 67.5 points.

## Exceptions
Exceptions to the late work policy require prior approval (see `policy_extensions.md`). Retroactive extensions are not granted.

## Contact
Contact your TA first for questions about a specific assignment. Email the instructor for policy disputes.
