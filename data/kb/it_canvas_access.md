# Canvas Access — IT Help

**Document ID:** it_canvas_access.md

## Overview
Canvas is the Learning Management System (LMS) used for course materials, assignments, announcements, and grades.

## Accessing Canvas
- URL: `https://canvas.jhu.edu`
- Log in with your **JHED ID** and password.
- Two-factor authentication (DUO) is required.

## Common Issues

### Can't log in
- Confirm your JHED account is active at `https://my.jhu.edu`.
- Reset your password at `https://my.jhu.edu` > Account > Password.
- If locked out, contact the IT Help Desk (see below).

### Course not appearing
- New course enrollments can take **up to 24 hours** to appear in Canvas.
- Confirm you are enrolled by checking SIS (`https://sis.jhu.edu`).
- Instructors can manually add students; contact your instructor if the wait exceeds 48 hours.

### Assignment not submitting
- Check the assignment deadline; Canvas will reject submissions after the close date.
- Supported file types vary per assignment — confirm format requirements.
- Try a different browser (Chrome and Firefox are best supported).
- Clear browser cache and cookies if Canvas behaves strangely.

### Video / media not loading
- Media hosted on Canvas requires a stable internet connection; try the JHU VPN if off-campus (see `it_vpn_access.md`).
- Disable browser extensions temporarily (ad blockers can interfere).

## Mobile App
Canvas has iOS and Android apps. Download from the App Store / Google Play and use `jhu` as your institution identifier.

## Getting Help
- JHU IT Help Desk: help@jhu.edu | 410-516-4357
- Canvas 24/7 Support: available through the **Help** link inside Canvas (bottom-left sidebar)
